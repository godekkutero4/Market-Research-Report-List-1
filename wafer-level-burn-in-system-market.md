<p><p>The Wafer Level Burn-in System market comprises three main types: Single Wafer, Multi-Wafer, and Full Wafer systems. Single Wafer systems focus on testing one wafer at a time, offering high precision but lower throughput. Multi-Wafer systems allow simultaneous testing of multiple wafers, enhancing efficiency and reducing processing time. Full Wafer systems treat an entire wafer collectively, optimizing throughput for high-volume production. Each type caters to different production needs, balancing test accuracy, speed, and cost.</p></p>
