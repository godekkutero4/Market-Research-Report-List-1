<p><p>The whole-house humidifier market primarily features two main types: warm mist humidifiers and cool mist humidifiers. Warm mist humidifiers heat water to produce steam, which is then cooled slightly before being released, making them ideal for colder climates and helping to reduce germs. In contrast, cool mist humidifiers disperse a fine mist of water at room temperature, promoting a comfortable environment and preventing overheating. Both types are designed to enhance indoor air quality and comfort, especially in dry conditions.</p></p>
