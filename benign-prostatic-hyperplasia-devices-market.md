<p><p>The Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia Devices Market includes various treatment modalities for enlarged prostates. Transurethral Resection of the Prostate (TURP) is a common surgical procedure that removes prostate tissue. Transurethral Microwave Therapy (TUMT) utilizes microwaves to destroy excess prostate tissue, while Transurethral Needle Ablation (TUNA) uses radiofrequency energy for tissue removal. Laser Surgery employs focused light to vaporize prostate tissue. Prostatic Stenting involves placing a device to keep the urethra open. Other techniques may include innovative or less common methods for managing BPH symptoms.</p></p>
