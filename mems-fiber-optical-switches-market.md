<p><p>MEMS fiber optical switches facilitate the routing of light signals in fiber optic networks. The market is divided into two main types: single-mode fiber optic switches and multimode fiber optic switches. Single-mode switches are designed for high-speed, long-distance communication by allowing light to travel through a single optical mode, offering lower loss and higher performance. In contrast, multimode switches accommodate multiple light modes, suitable for short-distance applications with higher signal capacity but increased attenuation. Both types are crucial for modern telecommunication networks.</p></p>
