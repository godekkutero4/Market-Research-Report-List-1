<p><p>The bearing refurbishment service market encompasses two main types: ball bearings and roller bearings. Ball bearings, known for their versatility and low friction, are typically used in applications requiring high-speed rotation, such as electric motors and fans. Roller bearings, characterized by cylindrical rolling elements, excel in handling heavy radial loads and are commonly found in applications like construction machinery and automotive systems. Both types benefit from refurbishment services to extend their lifespan, enhance performance, and reduce maintenance costs.</p></p>
