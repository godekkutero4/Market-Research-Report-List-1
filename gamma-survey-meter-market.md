<p><p>The Gamma Survey Meter market is primarily divided into two types: Geiger Counter and Ion Chamber types. Geiger Counters utilize a gas-filled tube to detect ionizing radiation, offering rapid response and portability, making them ideal for field measurements. In contrast, Ion Chamber types provide high precision and stability in detecting gamma radiation, often used in laboratory and industrial settings. Both types serve critical roles in radiation monitoring, environmental assessments, and safety protocols across various sectors.</p></p>
