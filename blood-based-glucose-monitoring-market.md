<p><p>The blood-based glucose monitoring market is segmented into two main types: wearable blood glucose monitoring systems and tabletop blood glucose monitoring systems. Wearable systems are portable devices designed for continuous glucose tracking, utilizing sensors worn on the body to provide real-time data, enhancing convenience for users. In contrast, tabletop systems are larger, stationary devices typically used in clinical settings or at home for periodic testing, offering detailed information and often providing advanced features for data analysis and management.</p></p>
