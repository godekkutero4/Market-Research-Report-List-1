Comparative genomic hybridization (CGH) allows for the detection of chromosomal imbalances across the genome. Multiplex FISH enables simultaneous detection of multiple genetic targets, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and efficiency in genetic research and clinical applications.</p></p>
