<p><ul><li>Coarse Grind</li><li>Fine Grind</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer grinding wheel market is categorized into two main types: coarse grind and fine grind. Coarse grind wheels are designed for heavy material removal, suitable for initial shaping and reducing the thickness of wafers, utilizing larger abrasive particles for faster processing. In contrast, fine grind wheels are used for precision finishing, creating smoother surfaces with finer abrasives. This distinction allows manufacturers to select the appropriate grinding wheel according to their specific application requirements and desired wafer quality.</p></p>
