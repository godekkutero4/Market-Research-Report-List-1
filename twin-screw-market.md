<p><strong>The Twin Screw Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Co-Rotating</li><li>Counter-Rotating</li><li>Intermeshing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The twin screw market encompasses three primary types: co-rotating, counter-rotating, and intermeshing. Co-rotating screws spin in the same direction, enhancing mixing and material transport, ideal for homogeneous blends. Counter-rotating screws operate in opposite directions, allowing for precise control over flow and shear, suitable for specific applications. Intermeshing screws have a unique design where the screws interlock, promoting excellent shear and mixing capabilities, making them suitable for processing diverse materials efficiently. Each type caters to distinct processing needs.</p></p>
