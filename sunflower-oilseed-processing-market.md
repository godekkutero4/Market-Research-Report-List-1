<p><p>The sunflower oilseed processing market primarily consists of two types: mechanical and chemical. Mechanical processing involves crushing sunflower seeds using physical methods, such as pressing, to extract oil, resulting in a less refined product with retained nutrients. In contrast, chemical processing utilizes solvents, like hexane, to efficiently extract oil, producing a higher yield and more refined product. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences and market demands, balancing factors such as yield, quality, and cost in sunflower oil production.</p></p>
