<p><ul><li>Direct Type</li><li>Indirect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mass flow regulators market is divided into two primary types: direct type and indirect type. Direct type regulators provide immediate control over the mass flow rate by directly responding to pressure changes, ensuring precise flow management. In contrast, indirect type regulators use a feedback loop, measuring flow indirectly through associated pressure and temperature signals, which may result in a slight delay in response. Both types are essential in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food processing, to ensure accurate flow control.</p></p>
