<p><p>The Frankincense resin market has two primary types: lump and powder. Lump frankincense consists of natural resin pieces harvested from the Boswellia tree, valued for its aromatic properties and traditional uses in incense and essential oils. Powdered frankincense is ground from the resin, making it easier to blend into formulations for food, cosmetics, or skincare products. Both forms cater to different consumer preferences, with lump resin appealing to purists and powder serving convenience in applications across various industries.</p></p>
