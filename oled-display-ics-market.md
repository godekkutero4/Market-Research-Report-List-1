<p><p>The OLED display driver IC market is primarily divided into two types: Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) and Active Matrix OLED (AMOLED) driver ICs. PMOLED driver ICs control display pixels with a simpler architecture, suitable for smaller displays and lower power consumption applications. In contrast, AMOLED driver ICs utilize a more complex structure, allowing for higher resolution and performance, making them ideal for larger screens and advanced applications like smartphones and TVs. Each segment caters to distinct market needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
