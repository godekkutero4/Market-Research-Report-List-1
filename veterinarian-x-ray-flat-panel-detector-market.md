<p><strong>The Veterinarian X-Ray Flat Panel Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Conversion</li><li>Indirect Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Veterinarian X-Ray Flat Panel Detector market comprises two main types: direct conversion and indirect conversion detectors. Direct conversion detectors use materials that directly convert X-ray photons into electrical signals, resulting in high spatial resolution and faster imaging times. In contrast, indirect conversion detectors utilize a two-step process, where X-ray photons first convert to visible light, which is then transformed into electrical signals. This method typically offers more cost-effective solutions but may compromise some image quality and speed compared to direct conversion.</p></p>
