<p><p>The membrane keypads market consists primarily of two types: flat-panel and full-travel membrane keypads. Flat-panel membrane keypads feature a thin design, providing a sleek and low-profile interface ideal for applications where space is limited. They offer a seamless, easy-to-clean surface, commonly used in consumer electronics and appliances. In contrast, full-travel membrane keypads provide more tactile feedback with a mechanical push-button feel. These are typically utilized in industrial and automotive applications, where robust performance and durability are essential.</p></p>
