<p><strong>The Electroplating Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pretreatment Agent</li><li>Electroplating Additive</li><li>Post-treatment Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electroplating chemicals are categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Pretreatment Agents**: These chemicals prepare surfaces by cleaning and etching, enhancing adhesion for subsequent layers. </p><p>2. **Electroplating Additives**: These are used during the electroplating process to improve deposit quality, control deposition rates, and enhance properties like brightness and hardness. </p><p>3. **Post-treatment Agents**: After plating, these chemicals help in finalizing and protecting the coated surface, ensuring durability and resistance to corrosion or wear.</p></p>
